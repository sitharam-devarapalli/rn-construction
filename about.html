Who We Are

Company Overview

Raghunayaka Nirman was founded in 2007 with a vision to deliver quality construction across Andhra Pradesh. Over the years, we've grown from small resitiential projects to large-scale institutional, industrial & infrastructure company.

At Raghunayaka Nirman, we are committed to buliding lasting structures and long-term relationships Based in vijayawada & Eluru are a multi disciplinary construction company offering comprehens ve solutions for residential, commercial, industrial, infrastructural and institutional projects across Andhra Pradesh

With over 2 decades of proven expertise, we've earned a reputation for delivering quality construction with precision, professionalism, and timely execucion.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Raghunayaka Nirman was founded by my father, a highly experienced builder with deep knowledge of construction and a strong reputation for trust and quality. With over four decades of experience, he laid the foundation of our company on honesty, hard work, and client satisfaction.

Today, I carry forward that legacy with a new generation of energy and ideas. My vision is to grow Raghunayaka Nirman into a modern, professional construction company that blends experience with innovation — delivering dependable and value-driven projects.

Mission: To build strong, safe, and quality projects that make our clients happy and satisfied.

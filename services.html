Building Construction

Our post-construction services give you peace of mind knowing the site will be there for you even after construction. Building construction is the process of planning, designing, and assembling structures such as homes, offices, malls, hospitals, and skyscrapers.

Construction is essential for urban growth, better living standards, and infrastructure development. It includes residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional.

  • Timber and steel frame construction
  • Turn-key projects
  • Groundworks and foundations
  • Mechanical & electrical installations
  • External landscaping, car parks & paths
